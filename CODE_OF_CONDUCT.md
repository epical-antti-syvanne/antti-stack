# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Antti Stack is dry, sarcastic, and occasionally hostile toward operating models.

It is not hostile toward people.

Contributors are expected to:

- be direct without being cruel
- assume good intent until evidence makes that too expensive
- criticize ideas, systems, rituals, and code instead of individuals
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, slurs, and private-data exposure
- keep satire aimed upward or sideways, never at vulnerable people

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- personal insults or harassment
- discriminatory language
- publishing private information without consent
- using issues or pull requests for vendor flamewars
- attacking named individuals instead of the machinery around them

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, close, or remove contributions that violate this standard.

Repeated or serious violations may lead to blocking from the project.

No steering group is required.
